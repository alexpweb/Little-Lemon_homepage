Every Flavour Tells a Story

Do you love Mediterranean food? Then you will love the Little Lemon!
As soon as you get in, a softly breeze of delicious aromas welcomes you onboard for
a cruise of mouth-watering flavours around Naples, Cipro and Antalya

Mario & Adrian use only organic ingredients, often from Italy, such as their greenish lemons!
Almost everything is homemade, from spaghetti to bread - and of course, their stellar Limoncello !

Because of its high quality and affordable prices, the Little Lemon is the ideal place to hang out for for any occasion.
